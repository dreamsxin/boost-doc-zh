arithmetic.hpp 包含了 arithmetic 文件夹中的头文件。

  • <boost/preprocessor/arithmetic/inc.hpp>
  • <boost/preprocessor/arithmetic/mod.hpp>
  • <boost/preprocessor/arithmetic/mul.hpp>
  • <boost/preprocessor/arithmetic/sub.hpp>
